About Jazz

Jazz is a uniquely American musical genre, with a rich and fascinating history! The roots of jazz can be traced back to the Afro-American folk music traditions, work songs, and blues, as well as 19th century band music and ragtime piano playing. Some of the key elements of jazz include characteristic rhythm patterns, harmonic practices, and improvisation. Jazz has had a significant impact on other musical genres, such as classical music and popular music.

Our Mission
